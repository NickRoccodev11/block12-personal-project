the Pokedex

the pokedex is an app currently in development that will work in tandem with "PokeAPI" to provide users with pokemon stats, images, and sounds. Users will be able to choose pokemon for their roster and develop teams, swap out new pokemon, and store the data about their pokemon for later reference.

Key Drummer

Key Drummer uses the HTML audio tag along with query-selectors in Javascript to allow real-time drumming with your keyboard! drum samples include hi-hats, kicks, triangle, snare, claps, and more!
